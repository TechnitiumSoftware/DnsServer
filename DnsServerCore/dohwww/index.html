To set DNS on your router, enter:

1. Access your router’s settings, usually at an address like http://192.168.0.1 or http://192.168.1.1.
2. Find the DNS settings section.
3. Enter the BetBlocker DNS addresses as your DNS servers, then save or apply.
4. Verify your router has saved the settings correctly.
